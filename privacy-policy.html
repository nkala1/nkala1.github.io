Privacy Policy for Drive School Manager Last updated: June 2026 Drive School Manager is an app for managing driving lesson scheduling between driving instructors and students. Information We Collect The app may collect the following information: - Name - Email address - Phone number - User role, such as instructor or student - Lesson scheduling information - Lesson status information - Invite code usage information How We Use Information We use this information to: - Create and manage user accounts - Connect students with their driving instructor - Manage instructor availability - Allow students to request lessons - Allow instructors to approve or reject lesson requests - Display lesson status and lesson history - Support basic app functionality Data Storage Drive School Manager uses Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore to store account and scheduling information. Data Sharing We do not sell user data. We do not use user data for advertising or tracking. Data Access and Deletion Users may request account or data deletion by contacting the app support email.
